CITRIS UCM is built from the emergent networks of collaborative researchers. The program is led by Dr. Joshua Viers. Associate Director Dr. Erin Hestir leads the EDGE in Tech initiative at UCM. Program Director Dr. Leigh Bernacchi serves to connect community, industry, and academic partners. The work would not be possible without the creativity, skill, and dedication of a team of UC Merced students. Please visit our People page to see more researchers.

We host speakers, events, research meetings, and visitors. Please contact us via email to plan your visit, request parking, and more.

If you have any questions or concerns, please send us an email!
